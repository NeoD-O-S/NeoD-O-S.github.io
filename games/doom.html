How to play a mod

Firstly, you'll need to know that those mods are in .wad files, somethimes .pk3 but what we will learn is the vanilla way.

Download a .wad in the internet, a example is this one: Example map

Now that you have the .wad you will put in your doom folder, normally, to execute the map you do:

doom.exe -iwad Doom.wad -file mod.wad

Doom will open and then you'll play the mod
